## About Me

I am a Master of Data Science student at UBC with a previous background in corporate banking.

Before moving into data science, I worked in corporate banking with a focus on credit analysis, financial review, client evaluation, and risk judgment. That experience continues to shape how I approach analytical work today: not only from a modeling perspective, but also from the perspective of business context, uncertainty, and decision quality.

I am especially interested in work at the intersection of:

- data science

- applied machine learning
